About the Game

A House That
Remembers

Orphic House has been silent for decades. You return not knowing why — only that something buried here is yours. What starts as exploration becomes a descent into the orphanage's darkest chapters.

No combat. No escape. Only the weight of what happened here, and whether you choose to carry it or confront it.
