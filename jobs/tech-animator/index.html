Who Are We

FreakGang Interactive, a team of six developers — a mix of veterans and newcomers — working remotely from all over India on our first game together. We move fast, stay curious, and leave our assumptions at the door.

We believe great games are built by people willing to explore, experiment, and figure things out together — not by following some specific playbook!

About the Project

We are building a first-person horror game for PC & Consoles with Unreal Engine 5, currently named WITH (working title). A narrative-driven atmospheric experience with a strong focus on character performance based storytelling.

What You'll Do

  • Learn & Use our custom animation pipeline based on Reallusion Tools — Character Creator, iClone & ActorCore Library
  • Animate, Stage & Capture animated character sequences based on provided scripts and feedback
  • Help integrate clips into our game scenes in UE5, supported by our dev team
  • Most importantly, pick up cool new gamedev skills along the way — we'll make this journey, however short, a FUN one!

Who We're Looking For

Students or early-career animators genuinely into video games — especially horror, narrative, or cinematic experiences. Prior coursework or personal game dev projects are valued over a polished portfolio.

  • Pursuing animation, game dev, or a related field
  • Basic UE5 familiarity; iClone or CC4 exposure is a plus
  • Enjoys video games!

We'll guide you through the creative and technical process. In return, we want you to walk away with real hands-on experience building game content from the ground up — and some $$ of course!
