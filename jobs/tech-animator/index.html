Who Are We

FreakGang Interactive, a team of six developers — a mix of veterans and newcomers — working remotely from all over India (and occasionally abroad) on our first game together. We move fast, stay curious, and leave our assumptions at the door.

We believe great games are built by people willing to explore, experiment, and figure things out together — not by following some specific playbook!

About the Project

We are building a first-person horror game for PC & Consoles with Unreal Engine 5, currently named WITH (working title). It's a Narrative-driven atmospheric experience with a strong focus on character performance based storytelling.

What You'll Do

  • Learn & Use our custom animation production pipeline based on Reallusion Tools i.e Character Creator, iClone & ActorCore Library
  • Animate, Stage & Capture animated character sequences based on provided scripts and feedback
  • Help integrate clips and sequences into our game scenes in UE, supported by our dev team.
  • Most importantly, pickup some cool new gamedev skills and tricks along the way for road ahead! We will make sure to keep this journey, however short, a FUN one!

Who We're Looking For

Students or early-career animators who are genuinely into video games — especially horror, narrative, or cinematic experiences. Prior coursework or personal game dev projects are valued over a polished portfolio.

  • Pursuing animation, game dev, or a related field
  • Basic UE5 familiarity, iClone or CC4 exposure is a plus
  • Enjoys video games!

We'll hold your hand through the creative and technical process. In return for helping us move the game forward, we want to ensure you walk away with some real hands-on experience of building game content from the ground up and some $$ ofcourse!

The Process

01 · Apply
Submit portfolio or showreel — game clips, personal projects, coursework all welcome.

02 · Intro
We will review your submission and if it's something that matches what we have in our minds we would love to hop on a brief call to say our Hi and exchange some thoughts.

03 · Trial
If all good, we would like to invite you to spend a week working with us before we lock in for a longer ride. If for whatever reason, something doesn't click for you or us, we would like to extend a small compensation for your time and we will say our goodbyes!

04 · Start
If all good and fine through week 1, we exchange some more info and sign the contract, you drop into our discord ( yeah that's our workspace ) and we get the ball rolling, that's all!
